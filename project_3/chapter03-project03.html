Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Joan-Auguste-Dominique Images

The portrait of Madernoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806

Medium Oil on Canvas

Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
